What is deforestation?













Deforestation is when forests are cleared out which results in less bio-diverse ecosystems among several other effects. Causes include logging, mining, oil and gas extraction, and cattle ranching.


































Life on Land and Below Water















What is overfishing?








Overfishing is when more fish are caught than the population can replace. This is significant because it negatively impacts ecosystems, disrupts food chains, and endangers fish species.
